Meet Alpha Team

Alpha Team is a student-led tech group from the College of Information Technology. Our mission is to spread tech awareness among students and introduce them to the latest technologies. We aim to expand across Iraq, bridging the technological gap in the country.

Follow Us

Our Mission

To empower students with the latest tech knowledge and skills, making technology accessible and exciting for everyone.

Our Vision

To become Iraq’s leading student tech community, inspiring innovation and bridging the digital divide nationwide.

Core Values

Collaboration, curiosity, and a passion for learning drive us to create a supportive and innovative tech environment.

Who are we?
Alpha Team started as a group of passionate students at the College of Information Technology, united by a shared goal: to make technology accessible and exciting for all. What makes us unique is our commitment to community, our drive to innovate, and our vision to expand tech awareness beyond university walls and across Iraq.
